A Non-Fungible Token (NFT) Is A Unique Digital Asset Powered By Blockchain Technology. It Ensures Digital Ownership And Represents Real-World Objects Like Art And Many More. TNCARTUT

TNC Art Is The Creative Arm Of The Dubai-Based Blockchain Firm TNC Group. Launched In 2018, TNC Is One Of The Fastest-Growing Companies In The Blockchain Industry, With Over 500 Developers And Experts Providing Blockchain Solutions All Over The World.

Driven By The Passion And Creative Genius Of Over A Dozen Digital Artists, TNC Art Aims To Carve Out A Unique Space In The Hyper-Imaginative NFT World. And, With The Insanely Successful Lady Apes Collection That Earned A Coveted Spot On Opensea’s Top 10, We Are One Step Closer To This Goal.

It Is The NFT Space That Motivates Us To Pick Up Our Stylus — That Inspires Us To Render Color To Our Imagination. Powered By The Opportunities Of NFT Technology, Countless Talented Artists From All Over The World Can Now Tell Us Their Stories. And It Is This Space That We Want To Give Back To. Join Us In This Incredible Artistic Journey.

LADY APE COIN

LADY APE COIN(LAC) IS THE TOKEN AT THE HEART OF THE TNC ART PROJECT,

CRUCIAL TO THE ASSET VALUE GROWTH OF ALL TNC ART AND NFT OWNERS.

Lady Ape Coin will be airdropped to users who purchase LAC, BAC, or DNA NFTs.

Airdrop rewards will be calculated based on the type of NFT purchased.

1 LAC NFT = 500 LAC coins

1 BAC NFT = 1,000 LAC coins

1 DNA NFT = 2,000 LAC coins

The airdrop rewards will be distributed on this basis until the entire supply of 9 billion LAC coins has been exhausted.

Airdrop rewards will be limited to those who maintain ownership of their purchased NFT(s) for at least 3 days after purchase.

*The distribution calculations above may be subject to change - so stay tuned for future updates about Lady Ape Coin airdrops.
